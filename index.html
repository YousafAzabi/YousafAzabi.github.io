About Me

Name: Yousaf Omar Azabi

Position: Software Developer

Company: Geovation Hub

Introduction

Dr Azabi main interest is software development where he learned different programming languages. He has developed the first prototype of a vehicle tracking system in Libya in his final year undergraduate project. He worked as IT Support Engineer. and has worked part-time and voluntary roles in the field of teaching and web development. Now he works for Geovation as a GIS Software Developer since July 2018.
Edited: 22nd August 2018

Biography

Dr Azabi has obtained BSc in Computer Engineering from University of Tripoli in 2004. He was one of the talented students in the department and acheived highest grades in programming and data structure modules. He studied hardware and electronics modules in his course. He travelled to UK to presue his postgraduate studies. He joind City University of London where he obtained a MSc in Telecommunications and Networks and PhD in Photonics in 2009 and 2018, respectively. He got a distinction in his master and was granted a scholarship for his PhD from the University. His reacher field is FEM simulation of spiral photonic crystal fiber to study their unique properties. Moreover, he worked in on different simulations of fiber optics including photonic crystal fibers and waveguides.
